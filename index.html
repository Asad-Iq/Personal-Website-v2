01 / about

A bit about me

I'm Asad — a Physics student with a passion for understanding how things work, whether that's the laws of the universe or the engineering of mechanical systems.

There's always something on the workbench. Some weeks it's a coding project, some weeks it's a car on the driveway — usually because I convinced myself it would be a quick job. That's pretty much been the constant. When I'm not tinkering, I'm usually chasing stories across borders, lost in a topic for longer than i intended, buried in a good book or at the piano. And on weekends, watching Arsenal systematically dismantle my will to live. The common thread through all of it is a love of learning, building, and exploring — whether that's a new place, a new idea, or a new challenge.

Currently based in the Leeds, open to anything that involves solving hard problems with creative solutions!
